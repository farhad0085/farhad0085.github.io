About Me

Hey there, I'm Farhad, a self-taught passionate programmer who loves learning something new every day. It started in 2015 when I was 16 — a young guy with a deep thirst for programming. I fell in love with Object Oriented Programming early on. After exploring Java, PHP, C#, C, and JavaScript, I found Python — now my everyday companion. What draws me most is Python's elegant OOP model. I learned Django, one of the greatest web frameworks ever built. My clients say I have a PhD in Django 😄. I also know Flask, which is great for leaner projects. My curiosity then led me to React and Redux for frontend development. I believe wholeheartedly: "It's never useless to learn anything — someday, it will be useful for sure." I'll keep learning until my last day. Regards — have a nice day!
